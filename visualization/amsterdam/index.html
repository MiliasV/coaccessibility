Accessibilty and Co-Accessibility in Amsterdam

Toggle map layers on or off:

Walkable Street layer

The walkable street network from OpenStreetMap that is used in this project.

Housing Blocks layer


People's residences in a spatial resolution of 100x100m2 (CBS data).
  • Color: Number of accessible places within a 5-minute walk

  • Click: Info about the accessible places within a 5-minute walk

Places layer


Places collected from OpenStreetMap.
  • Color: Age diversity of the people who have access to each place within a 5-minute walk

  • Click: Info about the people who have access to the clicked place within a 5-minute walk
